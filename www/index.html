About Me

Technology That I Endorse/Use

Through my education and personal experience, I have gained exposure and experience with many different technologies in the past years. I always try to expose myself to new libraries, frameworks, and software movements because as an aspiring and new developer, that is important to me.

I do have a personal philosophy with new frameworks and tools, and that is: don't be too quick to jump onboard with all of the shiny new things that come out. Many times, it is better to let new tools mature and become stable before using; that way, you can avoid your application breaking and avoid any beta-bugs. Plus, not all new shiny frameworks and tools are good.

I mainly use two IDE/code editors when developing, depending on the type of solution I am creating. I chose to use Visual Studio© 2015 Community when doing any type of ASP.NET, C# projects. I also choose to use Brackets© http://brackets.io/ when I am developing basic webpages, JavaScript apps, Node.JS projects, and such. If you haven't heard of Brackets before, take a look! It is a great, lightweight text editor with tons of free plugins.
